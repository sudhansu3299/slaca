L3 Audit Policy

κ

Metric

Scoring consistency on the L2 resolution label.

1

Poor

κ < 0.40 → rubric unreliable, tighten L2 definition.

2

Moderate

0.40 ≤ κ < 0.60 → review disagreement cases and re-anchor.

3

Acceptable

κ ≥ 0.60 → current L2 rubric is stable enough.
